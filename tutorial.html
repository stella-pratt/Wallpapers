1. Download a wallpaper

Find your desired wallpaper then click the green download button in the bottom right of the image. Then simply choose the size you want. If you are unsure what size you need have a look at the navigation bar to see the recommended resolution for your device.

2. Install your wallpaper

Now locate your downloaded wallpaper and right-click on the file. If you are using a trackpad, click with two fingers. With the context menu open, locate and click the "Set as desktop background" option. Now your desktop has a fresh look!
